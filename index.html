Registers Matter for Pixel-Space Diffusion Transformers

Nikita Starodubtsev, Ilia Sudakov, Ilya Drobyshevskiy, Artem Babenko, Dmitry Baranchuk

arXiv 2026

We show that registers can improve DiTs, especially in pixel-space models, and help explain some of their strong performance.

  1. Pixel-space models benefit the most from registers, while latent-space models show smaller gains or slight drops.
  2. Registers reduce patch-token feature norms and make intermediate feature maps smoother, especially at high noise levels.
  3. Recent pixel-space DiTs already seem to use register-like behavior implicitly.
